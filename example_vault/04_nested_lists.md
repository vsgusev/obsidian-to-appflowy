# Nested Lists

## Nested bullets

- Level 1
  - Level 2
    - Level 3
  - Back to level 2
- Another level 1

## Nested numbered

1. First
   1. Sub-first
   2. Sub-second
2. Second
   1. Sub-first

## Mixed nesting

- Bullet parent
  1. Numbered child
  2. Another numbered child
- Another bullet parent
